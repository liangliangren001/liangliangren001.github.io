创作理念 Creative Ideas

我的艺术创作由早期的实验动画转向纸艺装置，如今延伸至公共艺术领域。创作多以"文化图腾"、"宗教信仰"、"身体与环境的渗透关系"以及"意识边界"为灵感来源。通过事物之间的临在发生与冲撞，守株待兔某一个视觉先知的降临（此为艺术家创作时的"拟人化"方法论）。如今她的艺术实践更多更地集中于发现每件事物"良善"的质地，以及推向极致的"良善"所可能呈现的种种面貌。

My art practice has transitioned from experimental animation to paper art installations, and now extends into public art. My works are inspired by "cultural totem", "religious beliefs", "the relationship between the body and the environment", and "the boundary of consciousness". Through the occurrence and collision of things, I wait for the arrival of a visual prophet (this is the "anthropomorphic" method of artists). Now her art practice is more focused on discovering the "goodness" of each thing and the various faces it can present when pushed to the extreme.
